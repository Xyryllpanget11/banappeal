Support

How do I file a ban appeal?

To initiate the process of appealing a ban, we kindly request that you visit the designated ban appeals page and carefully select the server on which you wish to submit an appeal. Upon completing this step, you will be directed to an external platform where you will be required to fill out an appeal form, providing relevant information pertaining to your case.

How long does it take to process a ban appeal?

It is a standard practice for our team to diligently process ban appeals within a time frame of 24-48 hours. However, it is important to note that the duration of this process may vary depending on the volume of appeals we receive. We kindly request that you exercise patience during this period and refrain from submitting multiple appeals for the same ban as doing so will only prolong the process and delay the resolution of your appeal.

What should I do if my ban appeal is denied?

In the event that your ban appeal is denied, we respectfully request that you refrain from submitting any further appeals. Nevertheless, you may reach out to us to discuss the reason for your ban and explore the possibility of any additional actions that can be taken. Please be aware that we are unable to reverse the decision.

How do I apply?

Greetings, incorporating our server into your network is a straightforward process. To initiate this process, kindly proceed by clicking here to submit your application. Upon completion of the form, we shall receive an immediate notification, and we will respond to your request as soon as feasible via our support server. To receive timely updates on the status of your application, please join our Discord server via this link: https://discord.gg/appeal. It is recommended that you familiarize yourself with our website's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy before applying.

I got banned but didn't get a DM

Just so you know, sometimes the DMs don't work because you need to be in our Support Server and have your Direct Messages turned on. If you are in the Support Server and DMs are on, please check that the bot is online and working. But even if it's not, you can still appeal your ban. If you need help or if the bot isn't doing its job properly, you can always open a ticket on our Support Server. We're here to help you out and get you back in the game as soon as possible!
