By adding DBA to your server, interacting with DBA, and being in a server with DBA, you agree to these Terms of Service.

Section I:

The Discord Ban Appeals bot is not responsible for any damage or issues that may occur to your server in the event that the bot is used improperly. Therefore, you must use the bot only for its intended purposes.

Section II:

You must not use an excessive amount of commands in an attempt to overload or break the bot. If we observe any such behavior, we reserve the right to take appropriate action, including blacklisting your server, blacklisting you, or blacklisting any participants. We also monitor and log information for this reason, as explained in our Privacy Policy. Furthermore, you must not spam the bot's DMs, as this counts as a command. The bot is designed to listen for user commands only.

Section III:

If you violate these Terms of Service, we reserve the right to blacklist your server, blacklist you, blacklist involving participants, and/or blacklist anything. We may also remove the bot from your server and force it to leave when added when your server is blacklisted. If we blacklist a specific user, that user will be unable to interact with the bot.

Section IV:

You agree that we may log any non-personal data related to Discord in the servers where the bot is present, including the user who is using the bot. For more information about this, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

Section V:

DBA requires a set amount of permissions to work as intended. While you can edit these permissions, doing so may affect the bot's functionality. We require that the "/appeal" and "/about" commands are accessible to everyone. If we observe that these commands are inaccessible, we may kick the bot from your server, blacklist you, and/or terminate your ban appeals altogether. Please ensure that the Discord Integration Settings are set up correctly, and all slash commands with DBA must be @everyone accessible.

Exceptions can be made for servers that use verification, which are allowed to deny the @everyone role the ability to use "/appeal" and "/about". If you have denied @everyone permission to use those two commands, you must set up your verification role (aka member role) with the ability to use the commands.

Section VI:

This bot is not intended to cause harm to you, your members, or your discord server. You may customize the bot's permissions to your liking, with the exception of Section V. The bot will not engage in nuking, raiding, or any other intrusive, harmful, or malicious behavior. If you observe any suspicious or abnormal behavior, please contact us immediately and remove all permissions from the bot.

Section VII:

This bot has features that may be considered annoying by some members. By adding the bot to your server and/or being in a server that has DBA, you agree that the bot can perform the following actions: DM the members of your server, DM you, and reply to your DMs

The bot will only DM you if you engage in specific actions, such as DMing the bot, getting banned from a server that has DBA, or getting unbanned from a server that has DBA. If you do not wish to receive DMs from our bot, please block it to prevent any further contact.
